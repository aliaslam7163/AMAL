Terms and Conditions for Becoming Visible

The following form once filled out and submitted, will make you visible to institutions that provide aid to the homeless. The questionaire at the bottom of the form is crucial into collecting the necessary information from you and many others like you, to help the local governement and local institutions into putting together concise plans which will help the general homeless population overall.


If you want to remain anaymous, you can check the checkbox before the 'Submit' at the bottom of the screen. This will make an anonymous but the information you provide by answering the questions is still very useful in helping the homeless population in general.


We at AMAL thank you for helping us, and we look forward to helping you.
